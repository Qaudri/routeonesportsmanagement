About Us

Route One Sports Management was formed in 2014. Since then, they have added to their professional team of scouts, consultants and intermediaries. Their team have successfully scouted and signed football players from non league to the highest level throughout the football leagues in the UK. These clients include football players from a each of the football leagues and academies below.

  • Premier League
  • Championship
  • League One
  • League Two
  • National Leagues
  • Academy (Categories 1, 2 and 3)
  • Free Agents​

They are able to ensure a smooth transition for clients that are free agents, to having full time employment at a club that is right for them. They can ensure that scholars are being offered (the very best) first professional contract.

Experience that makes a difference

With a professional eye to spot emerging talent and the all round experience for true pro talent. Route One Sports Management have a large network to make sure they each player is on the correct contract and at the right club. If a player is without a club they have the contacts to create opportunities for club trials. If a player is currently playing non league football they can invite professional clubs to asses and make a true judgment for all of their players. With a high success rate for finding each client the right club and making a pathway into professional football.
